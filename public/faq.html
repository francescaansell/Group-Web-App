eTherapy is an online therapy platform focused on providing individuals and families with a variety of services. We aim to make our services easily and quickly accessible, as well as private, secure, and trusted.

Our therapists are licensed and trained in their area of study. We have a variety of therapists, each with a specific focus. You may reach out to a specific therapist if you would like to begin sessions with them or you can use the survey to be matched with therapists that suit your needs. All of our therapists are listed on the home page for further viewing.

Each of our therapists are hired following an in-depth background check, ensuring that their lincenses are real and up-to-date. All therapists must complete several training sessions with eTherapy before being accepted.

eTherapy costs about $50 a session, billed monthly depending on the number of sessions you complete each month.
*Emergency Therapy Sessions include a $15 upcharge.


We offer several options:

  • Emergency Therapy: If you need to speak to your therapist urgently, you can attend an emergency therapy session within 24 hours of your request.
  • Weekly Therapy: This is the most common form of therapy: attend 1 session a week with your therapist.
  • Bi-Weekly Therapy: Attend a session every other week with your therapist.
  • Monthly Therapy: Choose a date each month to meet with your therapist.


Overall, therapy is a safe, secure, and trust-worthy way to recieve guidance and support.
therapy can be beneficial for many reasons, including: alleviation of emotional and psychological issues and making personal improvements in communcation, health, and function.
The therapists at eTherapy offer an un-biased, objective point of view when speaking to you about your issues.


Our therapists at eTherpy are trained to deal with many different client struggles. Some common issues that eTherapy can help with include:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Addiction
  • PTSD or other trauma-related issues
  • Stress
  • Grief
  • Bettering Communication
  • Self-Esteem
  • Self-Expression
  • Decision-Making

and much more.


We have three different forms of therapy:

  • individual Therapy(adult or juvenile): Meet with your therapist alone.
  • Family Therapy: Meet with your therapist as a family, including any children, partners, parents, and more.
  • Relationship Therapy: Meet with your therapist with your significant other.


The video chat feature will be set up directly between you and your therapist. During your scheduled time, you may access the feature to meet up with them. You can also mute your microphone or turn off your camera during your session if you need to.


You may not notice the positive affects of therapy until you are a month or two in. Often times, it may take a few sessions to get comfortable with your new therapist.
You should notice that therapy is working if you find inprovement in your mental and physical health.


If you do not feel as if your therapist is a direct match for you, that is okay! You can always apply to work with a different therapist by emailing us.
