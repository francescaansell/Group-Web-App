Attending Therapy

Choose a therapy option: Group, Private, Emergency

Group

Group Sessions are intended to be safe and collaborative environments for you and others to interact and talk about issues. Usually, these sessions are for lighter topics and help individuals manage stress and allow them to share strategies for they use to imrpove their well-being.

Private

Private sessions are intended to be more personal sessions between you and a therapist. During these sessions, you'll spend time with your therapist to indentify key points that may be causing disruption in your life. These consultation sessions are designed to allow you to set up sessions based on your needs, allowing you to schedule at your convenience.

Emergency

In extreme cases, you can schedule emergency sessions to receive immediate consultation from one of our therapists. In the event that they aren't available to assist you during this time we will work with you to schedule an immediate appointment with another therapist to ensure you get the help you need.
