eTherapy is a telemedicine service dedidicated to assitting people tackle their mental health issues. Finding a therapist in person can take lots of time and money. Some therapists may even not be able to get you in for weeks

Meet our therapists


Dr. Julie Adams

Dr. Adams graduated with a bachelor's degree in Biology from Penn State University. After she graduated medical school at Penn State Health Milton S. Hershey Medical Center. She is a lisenced Pysriist who specilizes in traditional treatment for anxitey, ADHD, PTSD, and panic disorders.

Dr. Maya Ellis

Dr. Ellis is a graduate of the University of Pittsburgh where she earned both her bachelor's degree and masters degree in psychology. She is a licensed therapist who speclizies in working with couples and familes to imporve communication skills. She often does outreach to children in the foster system.
